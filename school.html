Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)

President and Treasurer (2018-2019)

As a member of ACM, we learned about new technologies and held LAN parties once a semester as a club bonding activity! Most notably, during the fall semester, we prepare to participate in ICPC and in the spring, we open club meetings to learning about new technologies or invite guest speakers talk about technologies used at their company, job opportunities, resume help, etc.
